Code With Us

We know there are tons of talented tech professionals like you who never get an opportunity to apply their skills to public service. We want to make it easier to contribute to government's digital services and get paid - and do it in a way where you can focus on writing code, not contract paperwork.

Here's how it works...


Find an opportunity that matches your skills and interest

The acceptance criteria describes what you need you to deliver to get paid the fixed price.

Connect

Talk directly with the opportunity contact on GitHub to get more clarity. If you have suggestions, or think the price is too low, say so!

Apply

The proposal evaluation criteria shows how your proposal will be scored. Apply through the app. If you’re selected, you’ll be offered the exclusive right to work on the issue for a set period of time.

Do the work

Work collaboratively and iteratively with the opportunity contact. Commit work as early and often as possible to ensure you’re on the right track.

Get paid

Once the acceptance criteria is met and your code is merged, submit your invoice and expect payment within 30 days. Read more about payment options here.
